Instructions

Lightly fry the paneer cubes in oil until golden brown. Set aside. If using frozen peas, blanch them in hot water.
In the same pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. When they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for 1 minute. Then add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
Add all the spices (turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala) and cook for 2 minutes.
Add green peas and 1 cup water. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes until peas are tender.
Add the fried paneer cubes and mix gently. Add fresh cream if using, and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
