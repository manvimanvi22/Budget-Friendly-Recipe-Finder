Instructions

Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.

Step 2: Add chopped onion, green chili, and coriander leaves to the dry mixture.

Step 3: Gradually add water while stirring to make a smooth, lump-free batter with a pancake-like consistency.

Step 4: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.

Step 5: Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the pan and spread it evenly into a thin circle.

Step 6: Cook for 2-3 minutes on one side until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.

Step 7: Repeat the process with the remaining batter, greasing the pan as needed.

Step 8: Serve hot with chutney or yogurt. Enjoy!
